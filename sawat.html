THE SAWAT VALLEY


The most beautiful valley i have ever seen and by far the most easy to acess place in KPK. The motroways connect mingora(the base of SAWAT) to Islamabad. Tourism in SAWAT keeps taking hits sadly whether it is because of Talibans or constant flooding as the river SAWAT is extremely strong





THE USHU FOREST


The closest you can get to pinewood forests in pakistan is the USHU forest and it is an ideal place to camp at night.The large trees make up for a very photo friendly place so make sure to click a few pictures there





THE GREEN TOP


Just the peak behind the kalaam main bazaar is GREEN TOP , it is an extremely tricky drive and requires a powerful engine but the road is quite good , the destination ofcourse is worth it as it was the coldest point in sawat





MAHUDAND LAKE


Mahodand Lake is a lake located in the upper Usho Valley at a distance of about 35 kilometres from Kalam in Swat District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. The lake is accessible by a four-wheel drive vehicle, and is often utilized for fishing and boating





MALLAM JABBA RESORT


Malam Jabba is an incredible hill station in Swat, Pakistan. This place is famous for its breath-taking natural beauty, crystal-like streams, lush pine forests, ski resort, and a lot of other attractions.The ski resort in particular is very famous in winter for people in pakisatn as it is the only ski resort in the area





BLUE WATER POINT


It is the most beautiful place i have ever been to without any shadow of doubt.The lush green pastures, the distant farmhouse , the grazing cattle was all i needed to make me the happiest person in the world . I will never forget that scene till my last breath
